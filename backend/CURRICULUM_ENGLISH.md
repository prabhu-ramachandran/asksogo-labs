## üß≠ The "Sorting Hat" Assessment
*The AI starts a friendly chat. The starting level depends on the child's response.*

**AI:** "Hello! My name is Sogo. What is your name?"
1.  **Response:** *(Silence)* or *(Native Language)*
    *   **Placement:** **Level 0 (The Explorer)**
2.  **Response:** "Raju." (One word)
    *   **Placement:** **Level 1 (The Friend)**
3.  **Response:** "My name is Raju." (Full sentence)
    *   **Placement:** **Level 2 (The Storyteller)** -> *AI asks next question: "What do you like to play?"*

## üó∫Ô∏è The Learning Path

### ü•ö Level 0: The Explorer (Words & Sounds)
**Goal:** overcoming fear. Vocalizing first English sounds.
**Format:** "Point and Say" (Visuals are critical here).

| Module | Scenario | Interaction | Learning Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **0.1** | **The Magic Bag** | AI shows an item. "What is this?" | Nouns: Apple, Ball, Cat, Dog. |
| **0.2** | **Simon Says** | AI commands simple actions. | Verbs: Run, Jump, Sit, Stand. |
| **0.3** | **My Body** | "Touch your nose!" | Body Parts: Eyes, Ears, Hands, Legs. |

---

### üê£ Level 1: The Friend (Simple Sentences)
**Goal:** Creating connection. "I am", "I have", "I like".
**Format:** Basic Question & Answer.

| Module | Scenario | Interaction | Learning Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1.1** | **Introduction** | Meeting a new friend in the park. | "My name is...", "I am 10 years old." |
| **1.2** | **My Favorites** | Talking about food and games. | "I like Biryani.", "I play Cricket." |
| **1.3** | **Family Tree** | Showing a photo of family. | "This is my mother.", "He is my brother." |

---

### üê• Level 2: The Storyteller (Describing the World)
**Goal:** Observation and Adjectives.
**Format:** "Look at this picture and tell me..."

| Module | Scenario | Interaction | Learning Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **2.1** | **The Classroom** | Describing a messy classroom. | Prepositions: "The bag is *on* the table." |
| **2.2** | **The Market** | Buying fruits. | Adjectives: "The mango is *yellow*.", "It is *sweet*." |
| **2.3** | **Daily Routine** | "What do you do in the morning?" | Time/Verbs: "I wake up.", "I brush teeth." |

---

### ü¶Ö Level 3: The Student (School Survival)
**Goal:** Functional communication for the classroom.
**Format:** Roleplay with "Teacher" (AI).

| Module | Scenario | Interaction | Learning Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **3.1** | **Permission** | Asking to leave the class. | "May I go to drink water?", "May I come in?" |
| **3.2** | **Lost & Found** | You lost your pencil. Ask a friend. | "Did you see my pencil?", "Can I borrow an eraser?" |
| **3.3** | **Apology** | You forgot your homework. | "I am sorry.", "I forgot.", "I will bring it tomorrow." |
